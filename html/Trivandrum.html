Thiruvananthapuram


Thiruananthapuram, the jewel in the emerald necklace that Kerala is for the Indian sub-continent, must surely have Parasuraman been a “must see” destination for ages, long before National Geographic Traveller classified it as one. Surely long before Sage Parasurama , according to local legend, threw his divine battle axe from Kanyakumari to Gokarnam to west Kerala, God's own land, from the Varuna the sea god; before the times of Mahabali the democratic and just ruler of this wonderful land who was sent down to the netherworld through deceit. It doesnt take any flights of fancy to imagine that this land fired the imaginations of intrepid travellers and explorers like Columbus, Vasco da Gama, Marco Polo, Fa Hien, and quite possibly, countless others from the pages of history, recorded or not.


Tourism has contributed heavily to the economy of Thiruvananthapuram. The entire tourism package such as hill stations, back waters, beaches, lagoons, and wildlife sanctuaries are present in the district.Foreign tourists flock to Thiruvananthapuram, a major destination for chartered flights to India for medical tourism, as there are more than fifty recognized Ayurveda centers in and around the city. This is primarily due to Ayurveda's immense popularity in the West. Medical tourism is further promoted by world-class modern medicine hospitals in the city. Recuperation facilities are available at five-star beach resorts and hill stations nearby.World-famous Kovalam beach is in this district.

Places of Interest


SREE PADMANABHA SWAMY TEMPLE

The Padmanabhaswamy Temple is a popular tourism attraction for its stunning architecture, intricate carvings, and religious significance. Dedicated to Lord Vishnu, the temple is known for its gold-plated exterior and the sacred idol of the deity reclining on the serpent Anantha. Visitors can also explore the temple's numerous halls, pavilions, and sanctums, each showcasing a unique aspect of South Indian temple architecture. The temple's rich history, including its association with the Travancore Royal Family, adds to its allure as a must-visit destination for tourists in India.

MAGIC PLANET THEME PARK TRIVANDRUM

The Magic Planet Theme Park in Trivandrum, India, is a popular attraction for tourists seeking adventure and entertainment. Spread over a vast area, the park features a range of thrilling rides, including roller coasters, water rides, and simulators. Visitors can also enjoy a variety of shows, performances, and interactive exhibits, making it a perfect destination for families and groups. The park's lively atmosphere and colorful decor add to the excitement, while the food courts and souvenir shops cater to all tastes and preferences. With its fun-filled activities and diverse attractions, the Magic Planet Theme Park is a must-visit destination for tourists in Thiruvananthapuram.

VELI TOURIST VILLAGE

Veli Tourist Village is a beautiful and serene destination in Thiruvananthapuram, India, known for its picturesque lagoon, lush gardens, and recreational facilities. The village offers a variety of activities for tourists, including boating, kayaking, cycling, and fishing, making it a perfect destination for families and groups. The village also features a floating restaurant, souvenir shops, and a children's park, providing a complete entertainment package. Visitors can also witness the traditional Kerala art and culture through various events and exhibitions held at the village. With its peaceful ambiance and exciting activities, Veli Tourist Village is a must-visit destination for tourists in Thiruvananthapuram.

MUSEUM AND ZOO IN THIRUVANANTHAPURAM

The Museum and Zoo in Thiruvananthapuram, India, is a popular tourism attraction for its fascinating exhibits and diverse wildlife. The museum houses a vast collection of rare artifacts, including ancient sculptures, traditional Kerala art, and historical objects related to the state's culture and heritage. The zoo is home to a wide range of exotic animals, including tigers, elephants, and zebras, as well as indigenous species like the Nilgiri langur and lion-tailed macaque. Visitors can also enjoy activities like boating and toy train rides, making it a perfect family-friendly destination for tourists in Kerala.

KOVALAM BEACH

Kovalam Beach is a stunning tourist attraction in Thiruvananthapuram, India, known for its crescent-shaped shoreline and pristine blue waters. The beach is a popular destination for tourists seeking relaxation and adventure, with plenty of opportunities for swimming, sunbathing, and water sports. Visitors can also explore the local shops and restaurants that line the beachfront, offering a taste of Kerala's rich culture and cuisine. Kovalam Beach is particularly famous for its breathtaking sunsets, which draw crowds of tourists and locals alike. With its natural beauty and vibrant atmosphere, Kovalam Beach is a must-visit destination for tourists in Thiruvananthapuram.

SANKHUMUGHAM BEACH

Sankhumugham Beach is a pristine coastal destination in Thiruvananthapuram, India, known for its golden sands, tranquil waters, and stunning sunsets. The beach is a popular attraction for tourists seeking relaxation and recreation, with plenty of opportunities for swimming, sunbathing, and beach volleyball. Visitors can also explore the local markets and restaurants that offer a taste of Kerala's rich culture and cuisine. Sankhumughham Beach is particularly famous for its impressive sculptures, including a giant mermaid and a beautiful sunset sculpture, adding to the charm of the beach. With its natural beauty and serene ambiance, Sankhumughham Beach is a must-visit destination for tourists in Thiruvananthapuram.

SREE CHITRA ART GALLERY

The Sree Chitra Art Gallery in Thiruvananthapuram, India, is a must-visit destination for art lovers and tourists interested in Kerala's cultural heritage. The gallery houses a vast collection of paintings, sculptures, and other art forms, showcasing the evolution of Indian art through the ages. The exhibits include works by famous Indian artists like Raja Ravi Varma, as well as international artists. The gallery also features a library and an auditorium, which hosts regular cultural events and exhibitions. With its stunning displays and rich history, the Sree Chitra Art Gallery is a top attraction for visitors to Thiruvananthapuram.

VARKALA BEACH

Varkala Beach is a stunning coastal destination in Thiruvananthapuram, India, known for its scenic cliffs, golden sands, and azure waters. The beach is a popular attraction for tourists seeking relaxation and rejuvenation, with plenty of opportunities for swimming, sunbathing, and Ayurvedic treatments. Visitors can also explore the local markets and restaurants that offer a taste of Kerala's vibrant culture and cuisine. Varkala Beach is particularly famous for its spiritual significance, with numerous temples and ashrams located in the surrounding area. With its natural beauty, cultural richness, and spiritual aura, Varkala Beach is a must-visit destination for tourists in Thiruvananthapuram.

NEYYAR DAM

Neyyar Dam is a stunning tourist destination in Thiruvananthapuram, India, known for its picturesque dam, lush greenery, and serene ambiance. The dam is a popular attraction for tourists seeking adventure and relaxation, with opportunities for boating, trekking, and wildlife spotting. Visitors can also explore the Neyyar Wildlife Sanctuary, home to a variety of animals, including elephants, tigers, and leopards. The dam also features a crocodile farm, deer park, and butterfly park, providing a complete entertainment package for tourists. With its natural beauty and diverse attractions, Neyyar Dam is a must-visit destination for tourists in Thiruvananthapuram.
